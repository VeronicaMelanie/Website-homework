About us

We are a small music shop!

Our love for a particular music genre made us wish to live out of our passion for it.
We tried to form a band, but we unfortunetly failed, but never gave up on our rock'n roll dream!

That's how this store was buid, with love and passion for music. We are selling records of many bands. Rock bands such as Aerosmith, Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd and so on, we also sell metal bands, some of them are Metallica, Death, Sabaton, Testament, Mayhem, Creaton and the lis can continue.

If you love music as much as we do, we are waiting you to pay us a visit!

In the store you are allowed to use the pickup in order to play any vinyl you wish and if you are in the mood you also can read a book in the reading zone of the store while enjoying your favourite music.
